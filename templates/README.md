# Public templates

This directory is reserved for practical employer resources such as interview scorecards, onboarding checklists and record templates.

Every template must:

- work without candidate or customer personal data;
- state its purpose and limitations;
- cite the source of any Government requirement or calculation;
- include a visible last-reviewed date;
- avoid presenting general guidance as a contract, legal opinion or medical instruction.

No public template has been released yet.
