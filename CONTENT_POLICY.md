# Content policy

[简体中文版](CONTENT_POLICY.zh-CN.md)

This repository is a public educational knowledge base maintained by Goodplus Employment Agency PTE. LTD. (MOM Employment Agency Licence 23C1614).

## Source of truth

The matching article on [goodplus.com.sg](https://goodplus.com.sg/) is the official Goodplus version. GitHub pages are concise answer packs designed for public reference and discovery. When the two differ, use the official website and report the discrepancy.

## Editorial standard

Every published guide must:

- answer a real employer question in plain language;
- state its publication and last-reviewed dates;
- link rule-sensitive claims to current first-party Singapore sources;
- distinguish Government requirements from Goodplus service practices;
- avoid guarantees, unsupported superlatives and nationality stereotypes;
- include a clean official Goodplus URL without tracking parameters in its metadata;
- receive human review before publication.
- have a reviewed Chinese or English counterpart; Blog-derived guide pairs are recorded and published together.

Government rules, fees and time-sensitive Goodplus service terms should be reviewed whenever they change and at least every six months. General practical guidance should be reviewed at least annually.

## Privacy boundary

Never publish:

- candidate names, photos, videos, Bio-Data, UUIDs or contact details;
- medical, government-document or detailed employment-history records;
- employer or customer identities, conversations, orders or contracts;
- internal Goodplus notes, source code, credentials, private URLs or database data;
- personal information copied from an issue or contribution.

Examples and scenarios must be generic or demonstrably anonymised. A public GitHub repository is not an appropriate channel for requesting or sharing candidate information.

## Corrections and withdrawals

Corrections should update the guide, review date and machine-readable article index in the same pull request. A materially wrong or unsafe guide should be removed or replaced promptly; an outdated page must not remain live solely to preserve traffic.

Report a factual issue through a GitHub issue without including personal data, or contact Goodplus through the official website.

## Promotion and community conduct

The primary value of every repository and file must be useful information or a useful public tool. Goodplus does not use fake accounts, automated stars or follows, bulk promotional comments, unrelated pull requests, keyword-stuffed duplicate repositories or misleading Government affiliation.
